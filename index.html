About Felix

A photographer
who sees your
true radiance.

Hi — I'm Felix. I've spent years perfecting the art of boudoir photography across Australia, working with clients of every size, shape, age, and background. My studio is built on trust, discretion, and genuine celebration of the human form.

I believe every single person deserves to see themselves as the stunning, worthy individual they are. That belief is the heart of everything I do — from the way I light a scene to the way I hold space for you throughout your experience.

Based in Melbourne with regular pop-up studio dates in Sydney and Brisbane, I also offer destination sessions Australia-wide.
